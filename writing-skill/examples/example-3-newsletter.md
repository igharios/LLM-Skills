# This Week: Coaching & Constraints

Most coaching is prescriptive. A coach shows up and says "Do daily standups. Use story points."

**The better pattern is constraint-first coaching.**

Ask: What's slowing you down? Where's the bottleneck?

We worked with a team where CI/CD took 40 minutes. Once they fixed it (down to 8), daily standups became natural. The coach didn't prescribe standups—the constraint created the need.

**Here's the pattern:**
1. Find the bottleneck
2. Understand the principle
3. Let the team adapt

Most coaching skips steps 1 and 2. It jumps straight to "do this thing."

Good coaching always traces back to a single goal. Speed? Reliability? Hiring velocity? Once you know that, every recommendation should support it.

The teams that improve fastest ask: *If we fix this constraint, what becomes possible?*
